NovaAI Business Performance Dashboard

This Tableau project delivers a comprehensive business performance analysis of NovaAI Technologies, a rapidly growing AI company operating in a competitive market. Using interactive dashboards and descriptive analytics, operational efficiency, financial performance, customer behavior, and regional growth trends were analyzed. Key visualizations include time-series revenue tracking, product-level analysis, regional distribution maps, and churn regression insights. Major insights revealed 47.8% profit margins, Asia-Pacific as the fastest growing region generating USD 13.7M revenue, and the AI Platform as the top-performing segment. Strategic recommendations include accelerating Asia-Pacific expansion and implementing predictive churn modeling to reduce retention risk.
